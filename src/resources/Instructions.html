How To Play

How to play Street Fighter!

Instructions

The goal of the game is to drain your opponent of their health by attacking them. When your opponent's health reaches 0, their character will die and you win! You can change your character from the options in the menu bar on the top left corner of the screen.

Controls

Player 1 is the person sprite on the left side of the screen. Use the W, A, and D keys to move around the screen and jump. Use the F key to slash at your opponent, and E to fire shots. You can avoid their attacks by jumping over their head. After your opponent reaches critical health, they will power up, so you must be quick at all times!

Player 2 is the minotaur sprite on the right side of the screen. Use the arrow keys to move around the screen and jump. Use the O(letter o, not zero) key to slash your opponent and P to shoot. You can avoid their attacks by jumping over their head. After your opponent reaches critical health, they will power up, so you must be quick at all times!

Have fun playing Street Fighter! Good Luck!
